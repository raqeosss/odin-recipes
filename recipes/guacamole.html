Description

Today we bring you one of the best snacks for a movie night, the best snack for poker nights, a Mexican traditional sauce, a killer for friend gatherings. The one and the only: Holy Guacamole!

Info

Will be ready in: 30 minutes

Utensils required: Knife, Spoon, Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2~3 tomatoes (choosing different coloured ones can make the dish even prettier)
  • Â½~1 red onion
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Cilantro
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut your avocado in half and scoop all of it with a spoon. Mash it in a bowl until it is made into a cream. Put it aside.
  2. Dice the tomatoes and onion and mix them with the mashed avocado.
  3. Taste it and salt it as you see fit.
  4. Add the lime juice and mix it. You can also add a dash of vinegar if you're up for extra sourness.
  5. Finely chop some cilantro leaves and incorporate them into the cream.
  6. You can serve it alongside some fish, rice and beef and whatnot, but do try it in the traditional Mexican style with some Nachos!
